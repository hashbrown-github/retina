OOH Tracker

The OOH Tracker allows you to audit and monitor your Static Out-of-Home advertising campaigns. Get real-time visual reports from the ground and generate detailed Excel reports for actionable insights.

OOH Tracker

OOH Tracker is a proprietary tool designed by Retinaad Limited to ensure 100% quality assurance of any OOH campaign, whether on digital screens or poster-based static billboards. Like never before, this technology-powered compliance system is addressing the age-long problem of lack of trust and lower accountability which had hindered sporadic adoption of OOH/DOOH media as an efficient and effective means of advertising compared with TV, Radio and Digital.

OOH Tracker also provides value-added monitoring service for real-time and on-the-spot assessment of static OOH assets through a computer-enabled “PosterTrak” App on any device, including the mobile. OOH Tracker is user-friendly, error-free and available at discounted cost for agencies and advertisers to monitor and audit advertising assets shared by vendors from campaign start date, thus guaranteeing a 360 view of OOH campaign execution with a higher level of integrity.

Features

Real-Time Audit Activity

Audit on-ground activity in real-time with detailed visual reports, including images and videos. Access comprehensive reports through the dashboard or Brand Manager app for efficient review and oversight.

Brand Manager App

Download our Brand Manager app to access all your campaigns and track audit activities with real-time images and videos.

Excel Report Generation

Create detailed Excel reports of your campaign’s audit activities, including all images and videos links, for thorough analysis and record-keeping.
