Guide: Understanding Ratings in Your Activation Dashboard.

The ratings system in your activation dashboard helps you evaluate the performance of each activation and its locations effectively. Here's a detailed explanation of what the ratings mean and how they are calculated.

What Does the Rating Mean?

  • 0-4: Below Average

    Indicates that the activation performance is significantly below expectations. Key areas might need immediate attention and improvement.

  • 5-6: Average

    Reflects a satisfactory performance. There is room for improvement in some areas, but the activation generally met basic expectations.

  • 7-8: Good

    Shows that the activation performed well and achieved most of the goals. Only minor improvements are required for optimal results.

  • 9-10: Excellent

    Demonstrates outstanding performance, with goals and expectations fully met or exceeded. This activation can serve as a benchmark for future efforts.

How is the Combined Activation Rating Calculated?

The Combined Activation Rating is the average of all individual location ratings within an activation. This provides a holistic view of the activation’s overall performance.

Formula:

Combined Activation Rating=Sum of all location ratings/Number of locations.

How is Each Activation Location Rating Calculated?

Each activation location’s rating is based on the responses provided by the Field Executive to the checklist items selected during the activation setup.

Checklist Rating Rules:

  • Yes Response: Adds 1 point to the score.

  • No Response: Adds 0 points to the score.

  • N/A Response: The question is excluded from the total score calculation.

Formula:

Location Rating=Total Yes Responses/Total Applicable Questions (Yes + No)

This rating is then scaled to a value between 0 and 10 for uniformity.

Example: Location Rating Calculation

Checklist Questions:

  • Did customer sign-ups happen on activation? (Yes)

  • Were app downloads achieved at the location? (No)

  • Was the activation branding clearly visible? (N/A)

  • Were promotional items distributed? (Yes)

Calculation:

  • Total Yes Responses = 2

  • Total Applicable Questions = 3 (Excluding N/A)

  • Rating = 2/3 x 10 = 6.67

  • The location rating for this example would be 6.7.

Why Do These Ratings Matter?

  • Performance Tracking: Helps you identify strengths and weaknesses across activations and locations.

  • Actionable Insights: Provides clear feedback for improving future activations.

  • Transparency: Ensures a standardized method for evaluating the efforts of field executives.
