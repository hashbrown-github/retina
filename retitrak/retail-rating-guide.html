Understanding Ratings in Your Retail Tracker Dashboard.

The ratings system in your dashboard helps you evaluate the performance of each retail audit and its store locations effectively. Here's a detailed explanation of what the ratings mean and how they are calculated.

What Does the Rating Mean?

  • 0-4: Below Average

    Indicates that the activation performance is significantly below expectations. Key areas might need immediate attention and improvement.

  • 5-6: Average

    Reflects a satisfactory performance. There is room for improvement in some areas, but the activation generally met basic expectations.

  • 7-8: Good

    Shows that the activation performed well and achieved most of the goals. Only minor improvements are required for optimal results.

  • 9-10: Excellent

    Demonstrates outstanding performance, with goals and expectations fully met or exceeded. This activation can serve as a benchmark for future efforts.

How is the Combined Audit Rating Calculated?

The Combined Audit Rating represents the average of all individual store ratings within a retail audit. This provides a comprehensive view of overall performance.

Formula:

Combined Audit Rating = Sum of all store ratings / Number of stores.

How is Each Store Rating Calculated?

Each store’s rating is determined by responses provided by Field Executives on audit app to checklist items selected during the audit setup.

Checklist Rating Rules:

  • Yes Response: Adds 1 point to the score.

  • No Response: Adds 0 points to the score.

  • N/A Response: The question is excluded from the total score calculation.

Formula:

Store Rating = (Total Yes Responses / Total Applicable Questions) × 10

This rating is then scaled between 0 and 10 to maintain consistency.

How is PoS Compliance for Retail Stores Calculated?

PoS (Point of Sale) compliance is measured based on three key factors for PoS item: presence, quantity and placement.

Item Compliance Calculation:

For each PoS item:

Item Compliance Score = Presence Score + Placement Score + Quantity Score

Total Compliance Calculation:

  • Achieved Score = Sum of all compliance scores for all items.

  • Total Possible Score = Sum of possible compliance points (excluding "N/A").

  • Overall PoS Compliance (%) = (Achieved Score / Total Possible Score) × 100

How is the Day-Wise Rating Calculated?

The Day-Wise Rating is the average of all store ratings for audits conducted on a specific day. This helps in tracking daily performance trends and identifying areas that need attention.

Formula:

Day-Wise Rating = Sum of all store ratings for the day / Number of stores audited that day.

Why Do These Ratings Matter?

  • Performance Tracking: Helps businesses monitor the effectiveness of retail audits.

  • Actionable Insights: Provides data-driven feedback for optimizing future audits.

  • Transparency: Ensures a fair and standardized evaluation of field performance.
