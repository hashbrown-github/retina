OOH Tracker

The OOH Tracker allows you to audit and monitor your Out-of-Home advertising campaigns. Get real-time visual reports from the ground and generate detailed Excel reports for actionable insights.

Features

Real-Time Audit Activity

Audit on-ground activity in real-time with detailed visual reports, including images and videos. Access comprehensive reports through the dashboard or Brand Manager app for efficient review and oversight.

Brand Manager App

Download our Brand Manager app to access all your campaigns and track audit activities with real-time images and videos.

Excel Report Generation

Create detailed Excel reports of your campaign’s audit activities, including all images and videos links, for thorough analysis and record-keeping.
