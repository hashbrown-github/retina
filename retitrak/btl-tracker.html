BTL Tracker

BTL Tracker is a proprietary tool developed by Retinaad Limited to ensure seamless monitoring and auditing of Below-The-Line (BTL) activations across diverse activation points. This innovative solution empowers marketers, brand owners, and agencies to maintain accountability and gain valuable insights into their BTL activities.

With BTL Tracker, users can set up activations and select or create custom audit checklists tailored to their activation needs from a predefined library. Audit reports are generated based on completed checklists and include geo-stamped images and videos of the activation location, providing real-time, reliable data and insights. These reports can be accessed instantly through the Brand Manager app for quick review and informed decision-making.

BTL Tracker also delivers detailed performance analytics, offering individual activation analysis, combined activation analysis, and day-wise activity assessments. Each activation report is assigned a performance rating, enabling users to evaluate the success of their activations. These reports, along with all supporting visuals, can be exported in PDF format for easy sharing and documentation.

With its user-friendly interface and powerful features, BTL Tracker ensures every activation is executed with integrity, delivering measurable results and actionable insights.

Features

Customizable Audit Checklists

Set up tailored audit checklists specific to each activation from a predefined library. Ensure your activations meet objectives and maintain the highest quality standards.

Geo-Stamped Audit Reports

Receive activation audit reports that include geo-stamped images and videos from the activation site, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Real-Time Report Access

Access comprehensive activation audit reports instantly through the dashboard or the Brand Manager app for efficient review and oversight.

Performance Analytics and Ratings

Analyse activations with individual, combined, and day-wise assessments, generating performance ratings for each activity. These insights are available directly through the Brand Manager app.

PDF Report Generation

Export detailed activation reports, including images, videos, and performance ratings, in PDF format for comprehensive analysis and record-keeping.

Brand Manager App Integration

Use the Brand Manager app to track all activation activities, access reports, and monitor progress in real time.
