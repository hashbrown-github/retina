Retail Audit Target Matrix & Checklist

Create a detailed checklist for field agents to follow during activations, ensuring all brand expectations are clearly outlined. Agents will use this checklist to capture real-time data and confirm key metrics at each activation site.

Section - 1: Presence, Material Quantity & Placement of PoS Materials

Printed Materials

  • Leaflets/Flyers (Handouts with product information or offers.)

  • Posters (Large printed visuals for walls or windows.)

  • Roll-Up Banners (Freestanding, portable banners.)

  • Standees (Life-sized cutouts or freestanding promotional boards.)

  • Tabletop Holders (Small stands for counters displaying offers or product info.)

  • Danglers (Hanging signs from ceilings or shelves.)

  • Shelf Strips/Talkers (Promotional strips attached to shelves.)

  • Floor Stickers (Adhesive visuals on floors directing customers or showcasing offers.)

  • Costume (T-shirt, Face cap or any other apparel for brand ambassador)

Digital Materials

  • Digital Signage (Screens displaying dynamic ads, videos, or promotions.)

  • Interactive Kiosks (Touchscreens for product browsing or placing orders.)

  • QR Code Displays (Printed or digital codes for accessing deals or product details.)

  • Audio Media (Music on play during visit.)

Product Displays

  • End Caps (Shelving at aisle ends for promoted products.)

  • Standalone Product Displays (Branded stands showcasing a product range.)

  • Dump Bins (Large bins for bulk product promotions (e.g., discounted items).)

  • Product Shelves (Racks or counters displays.)

Retail and Dealer Signboards

  • Outdoor Signboards (Branded signage for store entrances.)

  • Window Stickers (Adhesive promotional stickers for retail windows.)

  • Indoor Danglers (Hanging signs from ceilings or shelves.)

Experiential Materials

  • Sampling Stations (Tables or booths for product trials.)

  • Loyalty Program Ads (Materials encouraging loyalty sign-ups at the PoS.)

Section - 2

Brand Ambassador Engagement

    • Presence of Brand Ambassadors (Present at the assigned time, Absent during the visit)

    • Adherence to Script/Guidelines (Engaged with customers as per the script, Followed brand tone and messaging, Educated customers about promotions and products.)

    • Dress Code Compliance (Wore the correct branded uniform, Displayed appropriate badges/IDs.)

    • Activity Engagement (Actively engaging with customers, Demonstrated product usage or benefits, Distributed promotional materials (e.g. flyers, samples).)

    • Behaviorial Audit (Polite and professional demeanor, Positive customer interaction.)

    • Sales Effort (Promoted upselling or selling effectively, Captured customer feedback (if applicable).)

Section - 3

Adherence to Promotional Guidelines

    • Point-of-Sale (PoS) Material Placement (Materials are displayed in the correct location as per the planogram, All PoS materials are visible and undamaged.)

    • Correct Product Placement (Products are placed as specified in the planogram, Featured/promoted products are easily accessible.)

    • Promotional Material Availability (Leaflets, posters, or banners are present, Tabletop holders, roll-up banners, or standees are positioned properly.)

    • Product Display Quality (Shelf arrangement is neat and organised, Stock levels are adequate for the promotion period.)

    • Pricing and Offers (Correct pricing labels are displayed, Discounts or offers are clearly communicated.)

    • Planogram Compliance (Matches the uploaded planogram, All products are arranged as per the guidelines.)
