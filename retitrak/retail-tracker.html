The Retail Tracker enables you to monitor and analyze the performance of your retail branding and product displays across multiple retail stores. Gain real-time insights from the ground with audit reports featuring geo-stamped data.

Retail Tracker

Retail Tracker is a comprehensive tool designed to streamline the monitoring and auditing of retail environments. This solution empowers brand owners, marketers, and retailers to gain complete visibility over their in-store branding and product displays. With Retail Tracker, you can effortlessly track the progress of retail audits, products offtakes, shelving effectiveness, promotion attendance, branding status and access detailed, geo-stamped reports directly from the field.

The platform provides actionable insights into store performance, enabling you to evaluate retail audits store-wise, day-wise, and on a combined basis. This data-driven approach ensures that every aspect of your retail presence is monitored for compliance, effectiveness, and quality, thereby optimizing your overall brand strategy.

Features

Customizable Audit Checklists

Create and tailor retail audit checklists to your specific requirements. Adapt and update the criteria to suit varying retail environments, ensuring that every audit is aligned with your brand standards.

Geo-Stamped Audit Reports

Access comprehensive on-site audit reports complete with geo-stamped images and videos. This feature guarantees that every report is backed by accurate location data, ensuring transparency and accountability across all store visits.

Comprehensive Analytics

Analyze your retail audit data through a variety of interactive charts and graphs. Evaluate performance store-wise, day-wise, and in a combined view to uncover trends, identify areas for improvement, and make data-driven decisions that enhance overall brand performance.

Exportable Report

Generate detailed PDF reports that encapsulate all key insights and audit findings. Easily share these reports with stakeholders to facilitate informed decision-making and streamline communication across teams.
