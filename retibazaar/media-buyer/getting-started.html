About the Platform

Skip to Account
What is RetiBazaar?

RetiBazaar is an AI-driven OOH (Out-of-Home) advertising marketplace designed to simplify how outdoor media is planned, bought, sold, and measured. It brings together media buyers and media owners on a single platform supported by data, analytics, and intelligent recommendations.

Instead of relying on guesswork or fragmented information, RetiBazaar provides structured insights such as reach, impressions, traffic intensity, and audience context. This helps users make informed decisions while maintaining transparency and trust across the ecosystem.

Who is RetiBazaar built for?

RetiBazaar is built for two primary users: media buyers (brands, agencies, advertisers) and media sellers (media owners, operators, and publishers). Each user type has a dedicated dashboard designed around their specific workflows and objectives.

Buyers focus on planning, targeting, and performance insights, while sellers focus on listing inventory, managing pricing, and understanding how their locations perform. This separation ensures clarity without compromising collaboration.

What markets does RetiBazaar cover?

RetiBazaar currently offers OOH analytics and marketplace coverage across Nigeria. All insights, audience measurements, and traffic estimations are designed at a Pan-Nigeria level, enabling both regional and nationwide campaigns.

This allows advertisers to plan consistently across multiple cities or states while sellers benefit from standardized exposure and measurement across the country.

What types of OOH media are supported on the platform?

The platform supports both Static OOH (billboards, unipoles, wall wraps, etc.) and Digital OOH (DOOH) formats. Each screen type is handled differently to reflect real-world viewing behavior and exposure patterns.

This distinction ensures that impressions and reach estimates are realistic and tailored to how audiences interact with different formats.

How are reach and impressions calculated?

Reach and impressions are estimated using a combination of traffic flow patterns, surrounding activity levels, location context, and media characteristics such as screen type and venue environment.

Rather than using flat averages, RetiBazaar dynamically adjusts these values to reflect how visible and impactful a location is relative to others, providing a more realistic view of performance.

Do reach and impressions updated when campaign inputs change?

Yes. RetiBazaar updates these in real time as campaign inputs such as timing, budget, POIs, or audience preferences are adjusted. This enables users to test multiple scenarios instantly.

This flexibility helps advertisers refine strategies before committing.

What are POIs and why do they matter in OOH planning?

POIs (Points of Interest) represent commonly visited places such as shopping areas, transport hubs, religious centers, or entertainment zones. They help indicate where people naturally congregate.

By analyzing POIs around media locations, RetiBazaar provides context on audience presence and activity, helping users align placements with campaign intent.

What does traffic classification mean?

Traffic classification provides a relative indicator of how busy an area is compared to others. Instead of raw numbers, locations are categorized to quickly communicate visibility potential.

This makes it easier for both buyers and sellers to assess location quality without needing technical traffic data.

Is inventory data verified before going live?

Yes. Every inventory submission undergoes an admin verification process before becoming visible to buyers. This includes checking geo-coordinates, media specifications, pricing consistency, and visual references.

This review process ensures quality, accuracy, and trust across the marketplace.

Can RetiBazaar support both small and large campaigns?

Absolutely. RetiBazaar is designed to scale across different campaign sizes. Whether planning a focused regional campaign or a nationwide rollout, the platform adapts to your budget and objectives.

Users can start small and expand coverage as needed without changing tools or workflows.

Do I need technical or data expertise to use RetiBazaar?

No. While the platform uses advanced analytics and AI models behind the scenes, the user experience is intentionally simple and visual.

Complex data is translated into charts, tags, and recommendations so users can make confident decisions without technical knowledge.

How can I get support if I have questions?

RetiBazaar provides dedicated support for both buyers and sellers. Whether you need onboarding help, planning guidance, or technical assistance, our team is available to assist.

Buyer Queries

For Brands & Media Buyers

How do I start planning an OOH campaign on RetiBazaar?

Buyers begin by entering basic campaign details such as campaign duration, budget range, and targeting preferences. Instead of manual location hunting, the platform guides users through POI and audience-based planning.

Based on these inputs, RetiBazaar generates data-backed location options with estimated reach and impressions, allowing buyers to plan efficiently.

What are user personas and how are they used?

User personas represent audience groups based on lifestyle patterns, behavior, and movement trends. They simplify targeting by replacing manual demographic selection with ready-to-use audience profiles.

This approach helps advertisers focus on intent and relevance rather than managing complex demographic filters.

How does the AI recommendation page help me?

The AI recommendation page summarizes your campaign inputs and selected locations in one view. It visually explains how your choices are distributed across different activity zones and audience contexts.

This helps you understand whether your campaign is balanced, concentrated, or missing key opportunity areas.

What do cluster and cohort visuals represent?

Cluster visuals show how selected locations are distributed across different activity zones, while cohort visuals show the audience and POI composition around those locations.

Together, they provide a clear picture of where your investment is concentrated and what kind of audience exposure you are getting.

Can I refine my campaign after seeing recommendations?

Yes. RetiBazaar is built for iteration. You can modify inputs and instantly see updated insights and recommendations.

This allows you to optimize placement decisions before finalizing your plan.

How do I know which locations deliver higher impact?

Locations are tagged with traffic intensity and quality indicators, making it easy to compare options at a glance.

This helps buyers prioritize high-visibility areas while staying aligned with budget and audience goals.

Can I plan both local and nationwide campaigns?

Yes. The platform supports flexible planning at different geographic levels. Buyers can focus on specific regions or expand to broader coverage seamlessly.

This makes RetiBazaar suitable for brands at different growth stages.

Do I need to contact sellers individually?

No. RetiBazaar centralizes discovery and evaluation within a single platform. Buyers can focus on planning and insights while inventory management remains streamlined.

What insights do I receive after selecting locations?

You receive estimated reach, impressions, frequency indicators, audience context, and surrounding POI insights. These metrics help justify decisions internally and align stakeholders.

Is RetiBazaar suitable for agencies managing multiple clients?

Yes. Agencies can use the platform to plan multiple campaigns efficiently while maintaining consistent measurement and reporting standards.

How transparent are the recommendations?

RetiBazaar emphasizes explainability. Recommendations are supported by visuals and metrics so users understand why a location or area is suggested.

Can I speak to an expert before finalizing?

Yes. Our team can assist with strategy validation and optimization if needed.
