List Your OOH Media on Our Marketplace

Upload OOH Media inventory easily — reviewed and approved to ensure quality and buyer trust.

Sign Uptrending_flat

How Inventory Submission Works

  • Step 1

    Choose Your Screen Type

    Select the type of inventory you want to upload:

    Static Media – Billboards, Hoardings, Wall Wraps, Unipoles, Transit Media, and more

    Digital Media – LED Screens, Digital Billboards, and more

    View our Covered Media Types

  • Step 2

    Download the Template

    Download the Excel template tailored to your selected screen type:

    View Static Media Upload Template

    View Digital Media Upload Template

    Each template is designed to capture all required technical, location, and commercial details for faster approval.

  • Step 3

    Enter Your Unit Information

    Fill in your inventory details in the template, including:

    Location Details - Address, city, state, latitude & longitude

    Media Specifications - Media type, size, illumination, environment

    Pricing Details - Base price, availability period, loop details (for digital)

    Operational Details - This structured format ensures faster review, accurate verification, and quicker approval.

  • Step 4

    Upload Your Inventory File

    Upload the completed Excel template to submit your inventory for review.

    Each location moves to Pending Review

    Inventory is not visible to buyers until approved

  • Admin Review & Approval

    Our team reviews every submitted location to ensure accuracy and authenticity.

    Locations may be approved, or rejected

    Status updates are visible in your dashboard

  • Add Images & Videos

    Once your inventory is uploaded:

    Add real site images and/or videos for each location

    Visuals help verify the location and improve buyer trust

  • Step 5

    Inventory Goes Live

    Once approved:

    Locations become Active on the marketplace

    Buyers can discover, plan, and book campaigns using your inventory

    Inventory becomes eligible for campaign requests and bookings

Important Guidelines
for Sellers

Static and Digital inventory must be uploaded separately

One Excel row = One media location

Pricing and availability should always reflect real-world conditions

Updates to pricing, discounts, or availability can be made later via the dashboard

Only approved locations are visible to buyers

Why This Process Matters

  • Ensures high-quality, verified inventory

  • Builds trust with media buyers

  • Enables accurate planning, reach, and impression modeling

  • Reduces campaign disputes and delays

  • Improves overall marketplace performance

Need Help With Uploading?

If you need assistance:

  • Refer to the sample-filled Excel template

  • Contact support through the Seller Dashboard

Our team is happy to guide you through the submission process.
