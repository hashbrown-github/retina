AI-Driven Campaign Recommendations

See how your campaign inputs translate into smarter location choices, audience understanding, and investment guidance — powered by our recommendation engine.

Your Campaign Inputs, Interpreted

We begin by analyzing the details you provided—campaign timing, budget, location preferences, POIs, and target audience personas.

These inputs define the context for every recommendation shown on this page.

Where Your Selected Locations Sit in the Market

This visualization shows how your selected media locations are distributed across different performance zones within the chosen market.

Our model evaluates each location’s potential using real-world signals and groups them accordingly, giving you a clear view of concentration, spread, and missed opportunities.

What Exists Around Your Locations

This view summarizes the dominant environments and activity types surrounding your selected locations.

By analyzing nearby places and movement patterns, the model reveals the contextual makeup of your media selection—helping you understand where your message will appear and the kind of activity happening around it.

Where to Focus Your Media Spend

Based on your campaign goals and the performance characteristics of your selected locations, our AI recommendation engine highlights areas where investment can be more effective.

These insights are designed to:

  • Improve reach efficiency

  • Strengthen audience exposure

  • Guide smarter allocation across your selected market

Recommendations update dynamically as your campaign inputs change.

Who Your Campaign is Likely to Reach

This section summarizes the dominant audience groups present around your selected locations.

Our models assess behavioral and contextual signals to estimate audience presence, giving you clarity on how well your locations align with your intended audience.

Insights You Can Act On

All recommendations and insights shown here are generated to support faster, more confident media planning—without requiring manual analysis or guesswork.
