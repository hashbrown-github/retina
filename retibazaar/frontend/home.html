Secure & Transparent Transactions

A trusted payment and payout system designed to protect both buyers and media owners at every stage of the campaign lifecycle.

  • Payments are processed only after inventory is reviewed and approved

  • Buyers are invoiced strictly for confirmed and booked media

  • Funds are securely held until execution milestones are met

  • Payouts are released after Proof of Posting verification

  • Full visibility into booking status, approvals, and transaction history

Track Every Campaign with Retitrak

Seamlessly move from booking to monitoring. Retitrak provides campaign visibility, proof-of-performance, and on-ground verification—all integrated with Retibazaar.

View Retitrak

Retail Tracker

Monitor and analyze the performance of your retail branding and product display with our Retail Tracker. Easily track the progress of campaigns across multiple locations and gain real-time insights into campaign reach with visual reports from the ground.

OOH Tracker

The OOH Tracker allows you to audit and monitor your static Out-of-Home advertising campaigns. Get real-time visual reports from the ground and generate detailed Excel reports for actionable insights.

BTL Tracker

The BTL Tracker allows you to audit your below-the-line market activations. Track performance, manage multiple locations simultaneously, and analyze results with Excel reports for deeper insights.

Asset Tracker

Our Asset Tracker helps plan, monitor, and track billboard assets across multiple locations in real-time. It ensures effective execution, accurate reporting, and compliance with asset tracking. Access detailed insights to streamline management.

DOOH Tracker

The DOOH Tracker monitors Digital Out-of-Home campaigns in real time by tracking how often ads are displayed on digital screens. It provides AI-powered Proof of Play verification, performance insights across time periods, and exportable reports—ensuring transparent and verifiable campaign execution.
